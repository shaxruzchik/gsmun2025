About GSMUN

GSMUN (Global Summit Model United Nations) is an international MUN conference officially registered on MyMUN.com. Organized by the students of Oxford International School in Samarkand, GSMUN brings together over 80 delegates from Samarkand and regions across Uzbekistan to debate pressing global issues and craft innovative solutions.

Why Participate?

Public Speaking

Enhance your confidence and articulate complex ideas on a global stage.

Diplomacy & Negotiation

Learn to negotiate, draft resolutions, and build consensus like real UN diplomats.

Leadership

Take initiative, inspire peers, and lead committees toward impactful outcomes.

Conference Details

Date: 29 June 2025

Venue: IPU University, Samarkand, Uzbekistan

Delegates: 80+ high-school delegates from Samarkand and regions nationwide

Language: English

Committees: UNGA, UNSC, UNESCO, WHO
